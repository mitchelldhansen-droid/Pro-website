About Me

I've spent the last decade getting groups of people to build something together and then helping them explain it to everyone outside the room. Theatre directors, pharma clinicians, AAA studios, indie teams, students and their educators. Five industries, same job. Late last year I started teaching myself to code, because I kept hitting the same wall: I could advocate for my engineer's time, but couldn't say why in their language, and the people I was working with were paying for that gap.

Seven months in, I've shipped a 1,500-line Python text RPG (the one I built to find out what I didn't know), I'm running production and engineering on Project Zelena in GDScript with a team, and I'm co-developing a 3D cozy builder at Napkin Sketch Games.

What I'm looking for next is a team where production leadership, communication, and engineering fluency all pull their weight. Somewhere in games, ed-tech, or AI/dev-tools that needs someone who can run the standup, write the docs, and ship the feature, and where I can serve the people actually doing the work.

Engineering Breakdown

Player & Movement Systems
A 3D third-person character controller with progressive movement depth — step-up traversal, ledge catch, double jump, slide jump, surface-aware sliding, momentum preservation across mechanics, and cross-system interactions (slide-into-bounce, double-bounce timing).
Construction System
A grid-based building system with ghost preview, attached/detached placement modes, multi-cell pieces, rapid-build (horizontal + vertical) and rapid-delete modes, and MultiMesh rendering for performance at scale.
Physics-Driven Construction Objects
Specialized buildable objects with their own simulation behavior — trampolines with timed bounce mechanics, anchored fabric surfaces using procedural meshes and animated settling, and drape-on-geometry surfaces. Surface tagging drives player interactions.
UI & Input
Layered, controller-first build hotbar with category → piece navigation, status feedback, and motion juice. Designed for keyboard + gamepad parity.
Persistence
Save/load architecture with named slots and auto-save, including dynamic-object state.
Character System
Multiple rigged playable characters with per-character animation libraries.
