Visual Novel / Turn-Based RPG

Engineering & Production Lead Godot 4.6 GDScript Dialogic 2 Team of 6

Leading a team of 6 as both producer and engineering lead on a visual novel with turn-based RPG mechanics. Architected a modular autoload-based framework of 18 interconnected systems — from a persistence layer that serializes game state across combat, inventory, affinity, and narrative progress, to a d20-based combat engine with class-specific abilities.
